Speak out against Hamas' crimes against women

Hamas has waged a war on women's rights, committing heinous acts of sexual violence. On October 7th, women were subjected to rape, torture, and murder or abduction — not as random acts, but as part of a calculated strategy by Hamas. Evidence, including a Hamas glossary with explicit instructions for sexual assault, underscores this brutality.

International law in the Geneva and Rome conventions defines deliberate sexual assault of women as WAR CRIMES.

These actions, celebrated on Hamas’ social media and by their supporters, are not only war crimes but also despicable crimes against humanity. Yet, global outcry from women's rights groups has been violently silent. This silence is a tacit endorsement of these atrocities and a betrayal of the victims.

Many organizations have failed to condemn these acts or demand the release and protection of those still captive. This complacency allows the continuation of abuse and dehumanization based on faith and ethnicity.

We urge you to publicly denounce these acts. Stand with us in asserting that a woman's right to her body transcends politics. Silence and justifications are not options. Visit our website to see who has failed to act against Hamas' atrocities.
